About Our Process

We focus on transparency, clear communication and fair offers based on current precious metal prices. Whether you have a single gold ring or an entire estate collection, we strive to make the selling process straightforward and professional.

  1. Contact us by phone or text.
  2. Arrange a safe public meeting.
  3. We test and weigh your items.
  4. Receive a no-obligation offer.
  5. Get paid immediately if you accept.
